# Loan Book — Personal Loan Management App

## Product Identity
- **Name**: Loan Book
- **Tagline**: Every Loan. Every Payment. One Place.
- **Category**: Personal Finance / Loan Management
- **Platform**: Web application

## Summary
A personal loan management app for individuals to track, manage, and stay on top of all personal loans — whether they are the borrower or the lender — in one clean, organized place.

## Target Users
- **Borrower**: Individuals with active personal loans needing visibility into repayment timelines and due dates.
- **Lender (Friend/Family)**: People who lent money informally and need reliable records of outstanding amounts.
- **Dual User**: Someone who is both borrowing and lending simultaneously.

## Core Features

### 1. Loan Dashboard (Home Screen)
- Overview of all active loans — both owed and lent
- Total outstanding balance (split by borrowed vs. lent)
- Next upcoming due date(s) highlighted with amber accent
- Summary cards per loan showing: counterparty name, amount, due date, repayment progress bar
- Quick-add loan button prominently placed

### 2. Add Loan
- Form fields: loan type (borrowed / lent), counterparty name, principal amount, interest rate (optional), start date, due date, repayment schedule, notes
- Clean modal or dedicated page

### 3. Repayment Tracker
- Per-loan detail view with payment history
- Log a payment button — records amount, date, notes
- Running balance auto-updated after each payment
- Visual progress bar showing % paid off

### 4. Loan List / History
- Filterable list: All / Active / Completed / Overdue
- Sort by: due date, amount, name
- Status badges: Active, Overdue, Paid

### 5. Interest Calculator
- Simple and compound interest modes
- Inputs: principal, rate, term
- Shows total interest and total repayment amount

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, trustworthy, approachable — not intimidating
- Minimal, organized, precise
- Tagline: "Every Loan. Every Payment. One Place."

## Out of Scope (v1)
- Bank integrations or payment processing
- Credit score tracking
- Multi-currency support
- Business/team lending
