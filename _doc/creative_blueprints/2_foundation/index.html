The Case for Change

The Problem

Managing personal loans is fragmented and error-prone. People juggle repayment schedules across multiple sources, miss due dates, and accumulate late fees.

Informal loans between friends and family have no paper trail — disputes damage relationships.

Spreadsheets and notes apps are not designed for loan tracking — balances go stale and schedules slip.

No single view of total debt owed or total money lent — financial stress builds from the unknown.

The Solution

A dedicated personal loan tracker that gives you a real-time view of every loan you owe or are owed — formal or informal — in one calm, organized place.

Log any loan in seconds — with lender details, principal, schedule, and interest — and never lose track again.

Automated due-date reminders and a running payment ledger protect you from missed payments.

A full, timestamped history creates an honest record for informal loans — protecting relationships, not just finances.
