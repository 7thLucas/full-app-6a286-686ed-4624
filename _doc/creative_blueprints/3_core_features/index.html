What It Does

Core Features

Borrower Lender Dual User

Loan Dashboard

One screen showing all active loans, total outstanding balance, and the next due date — owed and lent in a single view.

Add Loan

Log any loan — lender name, principal, interest rate, start date, repayment schedule, and notes — in under a minute.

Repayment Tracker

Record each payment with a timestamp. The running balance and next due date update automatically after every entry.

Due Date Reminders

Scheduled notifications ahead of every repayment so you never miss a payment date or incur a late fee.

Loan History

A complete, sortable ledger of all past and active loans with timestamped payment records — your proof of payment, always available.

Interest Calculator

Built-in simple and compound interest calculator so you always know total repayment cost before and during a loan.
