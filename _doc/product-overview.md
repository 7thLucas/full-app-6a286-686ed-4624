# Loan Book — Product Overview

## Product Identity

**Name**: Loan Book  
**Tagline**: Every Loan. Every Payment. One Place.  
**Sub-tagline**: Every loan you owe or are owed — tracked, organised, and under control.  
**Category**: Personal Finance / Loan Management  
**Platform**: Web application  

## Product Summary
A personal loan management app that helps individuals track, manage, and stay on top of all their personal loans — whether they are the borrower or the lender — in one clear, organized place.

## Problem
Managing personal loans is fragmented and error-prone. Borrowers juggle multiple repayment schedules across different lenders, miss payment due dates, and lose track of outstanding balances. When loans are informal (between friends, family, or colleagues), there is no paper trail — disputes arise and relationships suffer. People lack a single place to see their full loan picture.

## Solution
A dedicated personal loan tracker that gives users a clear, real-time view of every loan they owe or are owed. Users log loan details, set repayment schedules, receive due-date reminders, and track payment history — all in one app. Whether the loan is from a bank, a colleague, or a family member, every detail is captured and organized.

## Target Users (Personas)

### Borrower
Individuals with one or more active personal loans (from financial institutions or informal sources). They need visibility into repayment timelines, outstanding balances, and upcoming due dates to avoid late fees and maintain trust.

### Lender (Friend / Family)
People who have lent money informally to others and need a reliable record of what was lent, repayment progress, and any outstanding amount — without relying on memory or scattered texts.

### Dual User
Someone who is both borrowing and lending simultaneously — they need a consolidated view across both sides of their finances.

## Core Features

- **Loan Dashboard** — A single overview of all active loans (owed and lent), showing total outstanding balance, next payment due date, and progress toward full repayment.
- **Add Loan** — Log a new loan with key details: lender/borrower name, principal amount, interest rate (optional), start date, repayment schedule, and notes.
- **Repayment Tracker** — Record each payment made or received, with timestamps and running balance. The app automatically updates remaining balance and next due date.
- **Due Date Reminders** — Scheduled notifications ahead of upcoming payment due dates so users never miss a repayment.
- **Loan History** — A complete, sortable ledger of all past and active loans with payment records — useful for reference, proof of payment, or dispute resolution.
- **Interest Calculator** — Optional built-in calculator for simple or compound interest to help users understand total repayment cost.
- **Lender/Borrower Profiles** — Contact-linked profiles for each person involved in a loan, making it easy to manage multiple informal loans with the same person.

## Key Benefits / Outcomes

- Never miss a payment due date
- Know your exact outstanding balance at any moment
- Maintain a clear record for informal loans — protecting relationships
- Reduce financial stress with full visibility into personal debt
- Save time previously spent manually tracking payments in spreadsheets or notes

## Positioning
A calm, reliable financial companion for anyone managing personal loans — formal or informal. Positioned as the simplest way to stay in control of money borrowed or lent, without the complexity of full personal finance apps.

## Brand & Tone
- Calm, trustworthy, clear
- Not intimidating — approachable for non-financial users
- Minimal, organized, precise
- Colors: deep navy primary, clean white backgrounds, amber/gold accent for payment alerts

## Scope

### In Scope
- Tracking loans (borrower and lender sides)
- Repayment schedule and payment logging
- Due date reminders
- Interest calculation (simple and compound)
- Loan history and audit trail

### Out of Scope
- Full personal finance / budgeting (not a general money manager)
- Credit score tracking
- Direct bank integrations or payment processing
- Multi-currency support (v1)
- Team or business lending
